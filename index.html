About Me

My name is Richard Peña and I created this webpage to showcase my programming abilities, integrate my interests, and learn to develop webpages like this one. I am handcrafting every element of this webpage, and plan to maintain this domain and webpage for the rest of my professional career. Users will be able to join me on my journey as I build upon the page, making improvements and tweaks, as well as provide feedback when the time comes. This webpage will speak to recruiters about my full-stack capabilities once it has been fine tuned. I am 23 years old and currently attending the University of California, San Diego. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor’s of Science in Computer Science and Engineering. I am a first-year transfer to the school, and it is my first time being away from home. My interests include building computers, java programming, video games, and finding great deals on computer hardware, albeit I can never afford any of these deals… I frequent Reddit quite often, and like to offer advice to those who ask.
